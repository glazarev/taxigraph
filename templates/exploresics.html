Explore data by SIC code


This interactive tool allows you graph the relationships between any tax-related variables of interest from the financial statements of publically traded companies and examine them by individual SIC codes. Each faceted scatter plot and histogram corresponds to a different SIC code. In addition to choosing what variables to graph, you can select the SIC industry codes of interest, as well as the fiscal years over which the data will be aggregated.
On the scatter plots, you can zoom in and hover over any company to get more details. The histogram dropdown menus allow you to create a custom ratio of any two variables and graph its distribution within the selected SIC code(s).
